SURVEY REPORT

By engaging directly with farmers, we were able to share our findings and offer practical solutions for managing plant diseases. The farmers benefited from the insights we provided, particularly in terms of disease prevention strategies and treatment methods. Our follow-up visits indicated that some of the farmers had adopted our recommendations, which contributed to improved crop health in the community.
The process of gathering, analyzing, and compiling data into a final report strengthened our research and analytical abilities. We learned how to organize field data systematically, synthesize it into useful information, and present it in a clear, accessible format for stakeholders. This experience in research and reporting will be beneficial for future academic and professional endeavors. .

CONCLUSION

In the final week, we completed the project by finalizing and distributing our report to farmers and other stakeholders. The follow-up visits to the farms demonstrated the practical impact of our work, and we were able to see the results of our suggested disease prevention and treatment strategies. The project concluded with a debriefing and reflection on the lessons learned, marking the successful completion of our objective to support the local farming community through practical knowledge on plant disease management.
